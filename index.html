  • Lan

    The Lan sect is renowned for their strict adherence to the rules. The sect is based on Gusu, in Cloud Recesses where their rules are engraved in a stone wall.They specialize in musical cultivation, both with the ability to keep up in battle or communicate with spirits by playing music. The sect was founded by Lan An with his cultivation partner. They have lectures which many prominent figures -previous and current- in the cultivation world have attended.

  • Wen

    Numerous minor sects have been consumed by the Wen Sect in pursuit of glory in ther bloodridden path. It's leader, Wen Ruohan was considered to have the highest cultivation nearing the peak towards immortality. Despite the ruthless and merciless acts done in the name of the sect, many people within the sect are against it. It is unfortunate that they have no power to stand up to the tyranny of the clan leader.

  • Minor Sects & Rogues

    A lot of cultivators not from the five great sects played great roles in the cultivation world. Some strived to be righteous, living by their ideals or adopting them from examples. Some have practiced unorthodox cultivation that either lead to devastating or fortuituous events. They all have compelling stories of their own, each worth knowing more of.

  • Nie

    The roots of Nie Sect of Qinghe trace back to a lineage of butchers. They are based in the Unclean Realm and they cultivate using sabres imbued with resentful energy. The price for using resenment however, is a short lifespan of the practicioners due to them being prone to qi deviations. The cause for their early untimely deaths is a closely guarded secret within the sect and they employ secret methods to suppress the resentment of their sabres buried in their ancestral hall.

  • Jiang

    "Attempt the impossible" is the motto of the Jiang Sect of Yunmeng. Members also use a clarity bell - a silver bell engraved with the signature 9-petal lotus of the clan - to clear their mind. They are based in Lotus Pier of Yunmeng, an area full of waterways, making the members excellent swimmers. The Jiang Sect also specializes in archery and members often train by targetting moving kites in the air. The fall of this sect had spurred the start of the Sunshot Campaign which aimed to take down the Wen Sect.

  • Jin

    The Jin Sect of Lanling is the wealthiest sect in the cultivation world, symbolized by the elegant peony "Sparks Amidst Snow." Based in the grand Golden Koi Tower, they are known for their opulence and political cunning. During the Sunshot Campaign, they delayed joining the conflict until the Wen Sect's defeat was certain, ensuring minimal losses while reaping significant rewards.
