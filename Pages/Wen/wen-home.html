WEN SECT

Countless sects have fallen due to the Wen Sect's pursuit of glory. Some absorbed into the sect, turned into a subsidiary sect, and others who lost their life in battle with the sect. The Wen Sect not only consists of powerful cultivators, they also have the advantage in numbers against other sects. Other than their notorious martial prowess, a branch of the family specializes in medicine. These people are mainly noncultivators with exception to the cultivator who was considered the top of her generation in medicine and her brother.

Location

This is the residence of the Wen Sect from Qishan. It is called a city due to its sheer size. Banquets and discussion conferences are also hosted here occassionally. An archery competition among the great sects was held here with no Wen Cultivator in the rankings. Wen Ruohan also has a dedicated place for his torture chamber, the Inferno Palace.

Notable Figures

Wen Ruohan's children were quite cruel and vicious like him. Wen Xu lead the attack on Cloud Recesses and burned their home as well as their libraries. Wen Chao on the other hand is very promiscuous and incompetent, only protected by his status - a spoiled brat abusing the power of his sect over others. Wen Zhuliu is Wen Ruohan's right-hand man and often guarded Wen Chao. He is exceptionally notorious because he has a technique that can melt the golden core of his opponent rendering them unable to cultivate again.

Wen Qing is a from a branch of the main family and is a Wen from Dafan mountain. She is a prodigy in the medical field. Wei Wuxian asked for her help after Lotus Pier was attacked and through her genius, she made someone become able to cultivate again.

Cultivation And Unique Practices

The sect leader, Wen Ruohan was said to have the highest cultivation at the time, nearing the stage of immortality. He can fight without weapons while only using qi with deadly efficiency. Only he was observed to do this using spiritual energy. His right-hand man is also special since he is also the only known person to have the technique to melt golden cores. He earned the title of "Core-Melting Hand" and he is very loyal to Wen Ruohan to a fault.

Wen cultivators wear white robes with red accents and on their sleeves are halves of a sun such that when they fold their arms together, it becomes a whole sun. Other than Wen Zhuliu's ability, there was no observed specialization in the Wen disciples. On Wen Qing's side of the family, they specialize in medicine. She uses needles as a weapon and also for treating patients. When Wei Wuxian stayed in the Burial Mounds, their fourth uncle was found to be great at making wine.
