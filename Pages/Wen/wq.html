Wen Qing

"I'm sorry, and thank you."

Wen Qing

Wen Qing, a prodigious doctor of the Dafan Wen Clan, was the daughter of Wen Ruohan's favorite cousin, under whose command she served as a healer. She was known for her medical skills and her oath as part of the Dafan Wen healers. She was fiercely protective of her younger brother, Wen Ning, and prioritized his safety above all else.

During the Sunshot Campaign, many wished for her to be assigned to the supervisory offices because of her comparatively kind treatment of others. Despite her reluctance, Wen Qing helped Wei Wuxian save Jiang Wanyin's life and later performed the Golden Core transfer for Wei Wuxian, transferring his Golden Core to Jiang Wanyin. After the Sunshot Campaign, Wen Qing and her remaining family were unjustly detained in concentration camps. She sought Wei Wuxian's help to escape, and he brought her family to the Burial Mounds, where they lived in hiding.

There, Wei Wuxian tried to revive Wen Ning, making him a sentient fierce corpse. When Jin Zixuan was accidentally killed, Wen Qing and Wen Ning surrendered themselves to the Jin Clan under the promise that the Burial Mounds would be spared. Before they left, she paralyzed Wei Wuxian, her last words being, 'I'm sorry, and, thank you'. However, the Jin Clan betrayed this agreement, executing Wen Qing and Wen Ning, scattering Wen Qing's ashes then resumed for the call for besieging the Burial Mounds.
