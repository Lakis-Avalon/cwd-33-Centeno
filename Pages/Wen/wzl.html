Wen Zhuliu

Wen Zhuliu, Core-Melting Hand

Wen Zhuliu, formerly Zhao Zhuliu, was Wen Ruohan's loyal subordinate who served him out of a debt he owed. He followed all orders without question, regardless of the harm caused, and was heavily involved in many of the Wen Clan's atrocities. He was Wen Chao's bodyguard, ensuring his safety and obeying his commands, even when they involved the slaughter of innocents.

Wen Zhuliu played a key role in the Lotus Pier massacre, where he used his ability to destroy Jiang Cheng's golden core, leaving him powerless. Despite his loyalty to the Wen Clan, Wen Zhuliu respected strong opponents, such as Yu Ziyuan, for their skill and courage in battle. He was not one to provoke fights unnecessarily but followed his orders to the end. He died defending Wen Chao during the Sunshot Campaign.

    Core-Melting Hand

    Wen Zhuliu has the unique ability to melt a cultivator's golden core rendering them essentially useless, unable to cultivate again. Not only that, he is also a great fighter able to take on many opponents.
