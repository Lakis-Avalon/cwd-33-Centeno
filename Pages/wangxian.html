Meeting at the Rooftop in Cloud Recesses

Wei Wuxian and Lan Wangji first met when Wei Wuxian attended the Gusu Lan Sect Lectures with other Yunmeng Jiang disciples. Right at the start, he already broke a rule by smuggling alcohol and not following the curfew. Upon reaching the gates to return, he was intercepted by Lan Wangji who is the sect's second heir and is a known stickler for rules. Wei Wuxian offered him a jar of Emperor's Smile.

Wei Wuxian's Shenanigans at the Lectures

Wei Wuxian despite knowing how to answer questions in lectures often got into punishments. Lan Wangji supervised his punishments in the library. Wei Wuxian often teased Lan Wangji during these sessions. They call each other by their birth names - a gesture which is very intimate, even couples do not do this often - Wei Wuxian slipped a yellow book into Lan Wangji's book. Before Wei Wuxian left Cloud Recesses, he gave Lan Wangji 2 rabbits which he took care of in the backhills of Cloud Recesses.

Wei Wuxian Touching Lan Wangji's Forehead Ribbon





The next time they meet is during the archery competition hosted by the Wen Sect. Wei Wuxian saw that Lan Wangji's forehead ribbon was askew so he grabbed it, trying to fix it. This made Lan Wangji upset because Wei Wuxian didn't know the significance of the forehead ribbon and how only family or spouses can only touch it. The author based this on the cliche that if the male protagonist sees the face of a girl or touches her in some way he has to take responsibility and marry the girl.

Working Together During the Wen Indoctrination

The Wens have attacked Cloud Recesses before the Wen Indoctrination. Lan Wangji attended with a broken leg. Wei Wuxian offered to help him but he refused. Later on, they got trapped with the Xuanwu of Slaughter because Wen Chao trapped them in the cave. The two of them worked together and managed to takedown this beast. They spent a few days inside the cave, both injured and Lan Wangji shared his worries about his sect being attacked and his family hurt. Wei Wuxian asked Lan Wangji to play a song for him to help him sleep. That was the last time they would see each other before terrible things happen.

Sunshot Campaign

Wei Wuxian went missing for three months around the start of the Sunshot Campaign. Lan Wangji and Jiang Wanyin looked for him during this time. When the forces of Sunshot Campaign were losing, fierce corpses appeared and attacked only the Wen Cultivators. Wei Wuxian appeared seemingly a changed man. Lan Wangji tells Wei Wuxian that cultivating resentful energy is harmful. Wei Wuxian takes it as Lan Wangji condemning him, and brushes him off. Through the course of the Sunshot Campaign, people think that they are not on good terms.

Lan Wangji Worrying About Wei Wuxian

"Wei Ying, come back to Gusu with me"
Wei Wuxian was able to control his cultivation with no problems, however, Lan Wangji always tells him to "come back to gusu with him". Wei Wuxian thinks that he wants him to come back to get him back to "righteous cultivation", but in truth, Lan Wangji wants to protect him.

Wei Wuxian Gets Kissed by a "Maiden" in Mt. Baifeng

The Sunshot Campaign has been won and the Jins are hosting a hunting competition. Wei Wuxian easily took 1/3 of all the prey so people got angry for him using "demonic tricks". He ended up wearing a blindfold to prove to people that he can do better than them with a handicap. He hangs up on a tree somewhere where he ends up noticing someone coming close to him. Wordlessly, he gets manhandled and kissed by this someone and before he could take off his blindfold, that someone's already gone. He tries to run after the person, but all he sees is Lan Wangji. Who he asks if he saw someone else who passed by, because the one who kissed him is definitely not the one and only Lan Zhan, Lan Wangji, Hanguang-Jun who always asks him to come back to Gusu with him. Lan Wangji says that no one else was there.

Lan Wangji Receives a Flower

Lan Wangji visits Yunmeng. He hears girls calling him from the 2nd floor of a building while throwing him flowers. It turns out Wei Wuxian is drinking with these women who turn out to be ghosts. Wei Wuxian gives him one of the flowers. Lan Wangji keeps said flower for many years to come.

A-Yuan's Rich Gege in Yiling

When Wei Wuxian and the Wen escaped to Yiling in the Burial Mounds, they became isolated. Once, when Wei Wuxian was shopping for essentials, he loses A-Yuan in the crowd. He hears a commotion and he finds A-Yuan grabbing onto Lan Wangji's leg. Lan Wangji asks Wei Wuxian who is the child and Wei Wuxian answers, "I birthed him". Wen Yuan ends up calling Lan Wangji A-die and Wei Wuxian A-niang.

Massacre of Nightless City

The sects backed out on their promise to leave Wei Wuxian and the remnants alone once Wen Qing and Wen Ning surrender after they killed them. Wei Wuxian confronts the sects since they immediately promised to siege the Burial Mounds the next day. People start attacking Wei Wuxian so he fights back. Many people die in the massacre including Jiang Yanli. Wei Wuxian lost control due to the emotional turmoil as well as him fighting for life . Lan Wangji despite the exhaustion brought back Wei Wuxian and defended him from his elders. He also confessed to Wei Wuxian but he did not hear it and forgot that Lan Wangji helped him.

First Siege of Burial Mounds

Wei Wuxian faced the four great clans when they sieged the Burial Mounds. Jiang Wanyin led the siege and the Wen Remnants got killed. Wei Wuxian died in the backlash when he tried to destroy the Stygian Tiger Tally. He hid Wen Yuan from the invading cultivators before the siege. When Lan Wangji heard the news, he rushed to the Burial Mounds with his heavily injured body - 33 whips from the discipline whip - and found Wen Yuan who he brought back to Cloud Recesses and raised. He gave him the courtesy name Sizhui which means 'to long for' or 'to reminisce'.

Reunion After Thirteen Years of Mourning

Wei Wuxian gets resurrected by Mo Xuanyu and fulfills his deal. He tries to avoid the four great clans but he encounters Jin Rulan who ends up in danger so he makes a flute and summons a fierce corpse to save him which was unexpectedly Wen Ning. After the fight, he tries to soothe him by playing a calming song from his memory but while he was playing this song, Lan Wangji grabs him and scrutinizes him. He then continues his playing and sends Wen Ning away. Lan Wangji brings him back to Gusu since he tried to bluff and say he likes Lan Wangji hoping to make Lan Wangji be disgusted of him. Unbeknownst to him, Lan Wangji knew it was him and brought him home.

Solving the Case of the Dismembered Corpse

They investigated the case of the dismembered arm that was unusually strong who appeared in the Mo Manor. They looked for other parts of the body it belongs to. All the time, Wei Wuxian tried to tease Lan Wangji, flirting with him more to make Lan Wangji fed up but none of them work. Lan Wangji was unfazed. When Wei Wuxian transfers Jin Rulan's curse to himself then faints, Lan Wangji slips up and calls him 'Wei Ying'. Wei Wuxian does not know how Lan Wangji identified him since Lan Wangji told him to find it out himself.

Tragedy of Yi City

The juniors observed that Hanguang-Jun acts differently around Wei Wuxian who they thought of as Mo Xuanyu. Lan Wangji was very protective of Wei Wuxian as they were fighting Xue Yang. He also comforted Wei Wuxian when they saw in a ghost's memory how Xue Yang mocked Xiao Xingchen who helped people and had good intentions become taken advantage of by Xue Yang and reputation got tarnished by the Jins for making the Chang clan take back their claims against Xue Yang. He mocked how his efforts to help people was useless in the end, which hurt Wei Wuxian as it reminded him of the Wens.

Drunk Lan Wangji

After the tragedy of Yi City, they stayed at an inn with the juniors. Earlier that day, Wei Wuxian asked Lan Wangji if he burned joss papers for him but Lan Wangji did not reply. When they both drank together that night though, Wei Wuxian discovered how much of a lightweight Lan Wangji is. Lan Wangji ended up acting a bit childish once he woke up again in his drunken stupor. Lan Wangji offered Wei Wuxian a lot of things like the toy he bought Wen Yuan, as well as money but when Wei Wuxian did not take them, he says that he won't burn joss paper for him. Lan Wangji did not because Wei Ying is alive. Lan Wangji also ties him up with his forehead ribbon and shows it to the juniors. The ribbon can only be touched by cultivation partners and family.

Wei Wuxian Identity Reveal in Koi Tower

They attend the conference in Koi Tower and investigate about Jin Guangyao. Wei Wuxian investigates through a paperman and sees Jin Guangyao possesses Nie Mingjue's head. Wei Wuxian's identity gets exposed and Lan Wangji defends him and stands by his side. Wei Wuxian asks him if he is sure and Lan Wangji shows that he is certain. Before they escape, Jin Ling stabs him on his side.

Wei Wuxian is Back in Gusu with Lan Wangji

Wei Wuxian wakes up in Gusu, in the Jingshi, Lan Wangji's personal quarters. There he finds out that Lan Wangji kept the sect rules with familiar handwriting as well as a peony flower as a bookmark. He reads about the clan rule about forehead ribbons. He also learns about the circumstances of the Lan family from Zewu-jun.

Second Siege of Burial Mounds

When they reach the Burial Mounds, they save the juniors. The people sieging the Burial Mounds still ended up blaming Wei Wuxian but this time, he was not alone. Lan Wangji was beside him and he refuted their accusations against Wei Wuxian. Later, the cultivators lose their spiritual cultivation and fierce corpses attack. Wei Wuxian and Lan Wangji made themselves baits for the fierce corpses and save the cultivators with the Wen Remnants who rose from the blood pool.

Golden Core Reveal

Wei Wuxian brought Lan Wangji to Lotus Pier to visit the ancestral hall where Jiang Wanyin confronts them. He kept insulting Wei Wuxian but when he moved to insult Lan Wangji, Wei Wuxian was angered. Before it became a full on fight, Wei Wuxian who bled from his orifices asked Lan Wangji to get them out of there. Jiang Cheng attempts to attack them but Wen Ning intercepts the attack. Fed up with Jiang Wanyin's attitude, he reveals the truth of why Wei Wuxian cannot do spiritual cultivation before, why he had to turn to his cultivation. He gave Jiang Wanyin his core.

Bathtubs Were Harmed in This Drinking Session

They then went for Yunping where Jin Guangyao suspiciously owned land. They had a drink together again and Lan Wangji was acting childish. Wei Wuxian then helps Lan Wangji bathe but does suggestive gestures and proceeds to kiss Lan Wangji. They break the bathtub, soaking the floor and leaking below them. They moved to the bed and proceeded to stroke each other towards climax. Lan Wangji snaps out of it and moves away from Wei Wuxian, who misinterprets it as Lan Wangji not liking it so he says that it's normal no need for it to mean anything. Lan Wangji then upon hearing this is also disheartened. They ended up on separate rooms and Wei Wuxian continues to investigate.

Lan Zan! Lan Wangji! Hangguang-Jun!

Wei Wuxian gets a revelation in the Guanyin temple under a hostage situation that Lan Wangji loves him. He learns of the 33 whip punishment administered on Lan Wangji for defending him against the 33 elders he injured. This is further proved when Jin Guangyao easily commanded Lan Wangji to disarm himself while he held a wire against Wei Wuxian's neck. Wei Wuxian, upon knowing this immediately blurts out his confession:

'Lan WangJi! HanGuang-Jun! Back then, I-I really wanted to sleep with you!'

Jin Guangyao is so shocked that he lets go of Wei Wuxian who immediately hugs Lan Wangji. Wei Wuxian tries to repeat his confession again but Lan Xichen stops him, stating that it is inappropriate. Further in the temple, Wei Wuxian apologizes for his poor memory and not knowing Lan Wangji's feelings, but promises to Lan Wangji that he really loves him, there can be no one but him and he can do anything to him, which Lan Wangji says back to him as well.

Marriage

After the events of the Guanyin Temple, Wei Wuxian and Lan Wangji eloped. Wei Wuxian also finds out that Lan Wangji was the maiden who stole his first kiss in Baifeng Mountain and teases Lan Wangji about it. They end up encountering Luo Qingyang with her husband and their child who Wei Wuxian gives money to. Lan Wangji's money, which he indulgently gives Wei Wuxian. They eventually return to Cloud Recesses but Wei Wuxian was quite unwelcome since Lan Qiren punished anyone who interacted with Wei Wuxian. Lan Wangji also do their 'Everyday is everyday', where they make love everyday.

A-Yuan is Alive

Lan Sizhui at the end of the events of Guanyin Temple remembers Wei Wuxian when he sees Chenqing and concentrates more. He recalled their moments together and Sizhui hugs them. Wei Wuxian was surprised becase he thought Wen Yuan died and is happy about this revelation. It served as proof that the things he did for righteousness resulted in something good.

How Lan Wangji Knew It Was Wei Wuxian

Wei Wuxian and Lan Wangji were travelling and Wei Wuxian plays the song he played during the Dafan Mountain to soothe Wen Ning and he remembers that it was the song Lan Wangji played for him. He apologizes that he did not remember and asks for the name. Lan Wangji says, it's WangXian, a combination of their names. He laughs when he hears the name saying how obvious the meaning of the song was if he knew the name. He liked the name Lan Wangji gave it.

Forgetting Envies

The characters in WangXian (忘羡) have specific meanings that reflect the essence of Lan Wangji and Wei Wuxian's relationship:
忘 (Wàng) - This character means "forget" or "to not dwell on." It is part of Lan Wangji's courtesy name, "Wangji" (忘机), which can be interpreted as "forgetting worldly desires" or "free from ulterior motives." This reflects Lan Wangji's disciplined and virtuous nature.
羡 (Xiàn) - This character means "envy," "admire," or "long for." It is part of Wei Wuxian's given name, "Wuxian" (无羡), which means "without envy" or "to not covet." It reflects his carefree and unrestrained personality, as well as his lack of concern for societal expectations or material wealth.

When brought together as 忘羡 (WangXian), the name can be interpreted as "forgetting envy" or "beyond envy." This conveys the idea of a relationship where both Lan Wangji and Wei Wuxian find peace, mutual admiration, and freedom from the constraints or desires that might otherwise burden them. It beautifully encapsulates the purity and depth of their bond.
