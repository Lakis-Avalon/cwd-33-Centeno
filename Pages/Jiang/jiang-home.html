JIANG SECT

Based in Yunmeng, the Jiang clan is known for their flexibility, resourcefulness, and a mix of strict yet nurturing leadership. Their motto, "Attempt the Impossible," reflects their tenacity. The clan emphasizes loyalty and the balance between freedom and duty.

Location

Situated on the banks of Yunmeng's rivers and lakes, Lotus Pier is a picturesque and vibrant base. Its design incorporates water elements, with docks and pavilions surrounded by lotus blooms. There are also many stalls which sell snacks near the pier and its disciples are often found frequenting them or swimming in the lakes. Despite its beauty, it is fortified to defend against attacks with arrays that need multiple cultivators to activate.

Notable Figures

Jiang Fegmian was the sect leader of the Jiang Sect. He was in an arranged marriage with Yu Ziyuan and they eventually had Jiang Yanli, then Jiang Wanyin. They had a tumultuous marriage because there were rumors of Jiang Fengmian loving Wei Wuxian's mother. After the death of Wei Wuxian's parents, Jiang Fengmian took him in as a disciple. This led to their marriage being more estranged as Yu Ziyuan's bitterness became more evident as she pitted Jiang Wanyin against Wei Wuxian.

Yu Ziyuan was notorious in the sect for her strict training and disciplinary actions. Oftentimes, she whips Wei Wuxian for minor slights. When the Jiang Sect was asked to be a Supervisory Office by the Wen Sect for the supposed insult to the sect by Wei Wuxian, she whipped Wei Wuxian in front of them as punishment and was almost compliant when asked to cut off his hand. She is an adept fighter respected even by the right hand of Wen Ruohan, managing to hold the invading Wen Sect cultivators for a long time while the massacre was ongoing in Lotus Pier.

Cultivation & Unique Practices

Jiang Sect disciples along with their motif of purple robes also have clarity bells hanging on their belts. This silver bell engraved with the design of the 9-petalled lotus helps clear the mind of the cultivator. Since Lotus Pier is full of lakes, most of its members are adept swimmers and are therefore experienced with dealing with water-based hauntings or beasts. They are also great archers as shooting arrows towards kites is a popular pastime. The sect did well during the archery competition held by the Wen Sect, earning placements in the ranking.

The spiritual weapon by Yu Ziyuan which was later inherited by Jiang Wanyin is Zidian. It is a whip that is in the form of a ring when not in use. Zidian is a whip of lightning that is not only a strong spiritual weapon in battle, it also has another use. Zidian has a remarkable ability to expel the spirit of someone possessing someone's body.
