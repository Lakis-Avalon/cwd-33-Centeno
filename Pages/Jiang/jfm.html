FORMER JIANG SECT LEADER

Jiang Fengmian

"A-Ying, A-Cheng... you must look after him."

Jiang Fengmian

Jiang Fengmian is the sect leader of Yunmeng Jiang. In his youth, he was quite close with Wei Changze and Cangse Sanren, Wei Wuxian's parents. There was a time Cangse Sanren saved their lives in a nighthunt because of Lan Qiren's insistence to follow rules. Eventually, Wei Changze and Cangse Sanren eloped and people thought Jiang Fengmian loved her. He later entered an arranged marriage with Yu Ziyuan from Meishan Yu Sect, known for its tough women. He had children, the first being Jiang Yanli, followed by Jiang Wanyin.

When he heard the news that Cangse Sanren and Wei Changze died, he looked for Wei Wuxian and brought him to Lotus Pier. This further worsened his estranged relationship with Yu Ziyuan, fueled further by rumors that Wei Wuxian is his illegitimate child. Wei Wuxian grew up with his children and he came to treat him like his own son. He often tried to instill the values of his sect on his children, "Attempt the impossible". He tried to bond with Jiang Cheng as seen in him trying to play kites with him but Yu Ziyuan made a stir so he opted to step out for lesser conflict. People perceive him favoring Wei Wuxian over his children but in his last moments, he asked Wei Wuxian to take care of them when he was on his way to return to Lotus Pier to try to defend it against the invading Wen. He ends up dying together with Yu Ziyuan holding his gift hairpin for her which implies there might be a possibility of them having feelings.
