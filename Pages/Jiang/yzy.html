JIANG SECT CULTIVATOR

Yu Ziyuan

"Madam Yu seized Wei Wuxian by the collar, looking like she wanted to choke him to death '...you damn brat!'. She hissed through clenched teeth. 'Hateful! How absolutely hateful! Look what disaster you've brought on our family!'"

Yu Ziyuan, Violet Spider of Lotus Pier

Yu Ziyuan, known as the Violet Spider of Lotus Pier, was a formidable cultivator whose reputation for mastery over Zidian and her resourceful use of the environment earned her widespread fear and respect. Even Wen Zhuliu, Wen Ruohan’s trusted enforcer, regarded her with caution. As the matriarch of the Yunmeng Jiang Sect, Yu Ziyuan held a significant role in maintaining the sect’s strength and discipline. However, beneath her commanding presence was a deeply bitter woman, whose unhappiness in her marriage to Jiang Fengmian manifested in her harshness toward her children, and mostly their subordinate Wei Wuxian.Yu Ziyuan's bitterness largely stemmed from her perception of Jiang Fengmian's favoritism toward Wei Wuxian, who was rumored to be his illegitimate child. This belief, coupled with her feelings of neglect in her marriage, drove her to treat Wei Wuxian with disdain, often reducing him to the role of a servant or even a "dog." She frequently pitted him against her son, Jiang Cheng, in an attempt to push Jiang Cheng to excel. However, Wei Wuxian's natural brilliance in cultivation only served to overshadow Jiang Cheng, exacerbating Yu Ziyuan's frustrations. She always thought that her family would be endangered because of trouble brought by Wei Wuxian, and in her final moments, she blamed Wei Wuxian for the fall of Lotus Pier and made him take the duty of protecting Jiang Cheng, even at the cost of his own life.

Her relationships with her children, Jiang Cheng and Jiang Yanli, were marked by strictness and tough love. Yu Ziyuan's intentions, were to prepare them for the challenges of leadership and survival in the harsh world of cultivation. She pushed Jiang Cheng relentlessly, believing that only through discipline and competition could he grow strong enough to succeed as the next sect leader. With Jiang Yanli, Yu Ziyuan showed rare moments of vulnerability, offering her daughter words of encouragement and urging her to be resilient in the face of adversity. This moment of maternal care revealed a softer side of Yu Ziyuan that was often overshadowed by her severity. Yu Ziyuan's friendship with Madam Jin also played a pivotal role in shaping the Yunmeng Jiang Sect's future, as she arranged Jiang Yanli's betrothal to Jin Zixuan. While her methods were often abrasive, Yu Ziyuan genuinely wanted what she believed was best for her children, even if her delivery caused pain. Her life ended tragically during the Wen Clan's attack on Lotus Pier, but even in her final moments, her love for her children shone through her actions and decisions.
