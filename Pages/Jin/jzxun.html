Jin Zixun

"Who is it again?"

Jin Zixun

Jin Zixun, a member of the influential Jin Clan, is known for his arrogance and self-importance, often using his family's power and status as a way to assert dominance over others. He looks down on people like Wei Wuxian, whom he frequently insults. One notable instance of this occurs during a hunt at Baifeng Mountain, where Jin Zixun criticizes Wei Wuxian for killing a large number of prey. His disdain for Wei Wuxian only grows over time, culminating in a significant conflict when Jin Zixun accuses Wei Wuxian of cursing him with the "Hundred Holes Disease." This accusation stems from his belief that Wei Wuxian is responsible for his suffering, despite there being no evidence to support this claim.

Fueled by this belief, Jin Zixun leads an ambush against Wei Wuxian in Qiongqi, as Wei Wuxian travels to attend Jin Ling's 100-day celebration. He sees this as a way to exact revenge, convinced that Wei Wuxian's supposed curse is the source of his misfortune. However, this action is based on a false premise, and the ambush only serves to further escalate the animosity between the two. Jin Zixun's life comes to a tragic end when he succumbs to the very curse he blamed on Wei Wuxian. His death, which occurs due to the Hundred Holes Disease, appears to confirm his accusations — at least to those who don't know the full truth. It is only thirteen years later that it is revealed that the true culprit behind the curse was not Wei Wuxian, but rather Su She, who had secretly cursed Jin Zixun, ultimately leading to his death.
