Mo Xuanyu

Mo Xuanyu was the illegitimate son of Jin Guangshan, the leader of the Jin Sect. His illegitimacy placed him in an awkward position, as he was neither fully accepted by the Jin family nor given any meaningful acknowledgment. Despite this, Mo Xuanyu was sent to Koi Tower, the Jin Sect's headquarters, where he trained as a cultivator. While at Koi Tower, Mo Xuanyu gained a reputation for being mentally unstable on top of being a cutsleeve. He was later expelled from the sect for having improper behavior towards Jin Guangyao

After being cast out, Mo Xuanyu returned to Mo Village to live with his maternal family. His time there was miserable, as he was treated cruelly by his relatives, particularly by his cousin, Mo Ziyuan, and his aunt. They considered him a disgrace and subjected him to regular abuse. Unable to endure their mistreatment any longer, Mo Xuanyu turned to demonic cultivation, a forbidden practice. Using a sacrificial ritual, he offered his own body to summon the spirit of Wei Wuxian, the Yiling Patriarch, to take revenge on his abusive family. Mo Xuanyu's sacrifice succeeded, as Wei Wuxian was resurrected in his body.

    Soul Sacrifice Ritual to Revive the Yiling Patriarch

    Mo Xuanyu like many people at the time believed that the legendary feared Yikling Patriarch was evil and sinister. In hopes of taking revenge on his family, he summoned Wei Wuxian by offering his body in exchange of him killing his maternal family who abused him. If Wei Wuxian didn't follow him, his soul would be torn apart. He did not execute the ritual properly as he successfully summoned Wei Wuxian, but didn't tell him the specifics of what he should do.
