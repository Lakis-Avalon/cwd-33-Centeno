Jin Rulan

"This is my father's sword. I won't put it down"

Jin Rulan

Jin Ling, the son of Jin Zixuan and Jiang Yanli, grew up as an orphan after both of his parents tragically died. His father, Jin Zixuan, was killed during a confrontation involving Wei Wuxian, and his mother, Jiang Yanli, died protecting Wei Wuxian during the chaos at the Nightless City. As a result, Jin Ling was raised by his maternal uncle Jiang Cheng and his paternal uncle Jin Guangyao. Both uncles had different approaches to raising him, and Jin Ling grew up as a spoiled child, receiving indulgence due to his status as the Jin Clan's heir and the absence of his parents.

The belief that Wei Wuxian was responsible for his parents' deaths fostered resentment and anger in Jin Ling. He grew up hostile toward Wei Wuxian, who was seen as the cause of his family's tragedy. This animosity persisted even when Wei Wuxian returned in Mo Xuanyu's body, and their initial interactions were marked by Jin Ling's harsh treatment of Wei Wuxian.

Throughout their encounters, Jin Ling and Wei Wuxian had a complicated dynamic, with Jin Ling often lashing out at him due to his hatred and unresolved feelings about his parents' deaths. However, as they spent more time together, Jin Ling reluctantly began to accept Wei Wuxian's help, though he still struggled with his deep-seated anger. When Wei Wuxian's true identity was revealed, Jin Ling's feelings became more conflicted. In a moment of rage and confusion, he stabbed Wei Wuxian, unable to reconcile his admiration for the person who had protected him and his belief that Wei Wuxian was to blame for the loss of his parents. Jin Ling eventually learned the truth—that Wei Wuxian had been framed for the events surrounding his parents' deaths and was not responsible for their tragedy.

Trivias

Courtesy Name
Jin Rulan

Wei Wuxian gave Jin Ling his courtesy name, Rulan. According to author Mo Xiang Tong Xiu, Jin Ling dislikes the name, considering it "sissy". It means "like an orchid"

Death
Supposed to be a Fierce Corpse

The author originally planned to make him die near the end of the novel and make him a fierce corpse but scrapped it because there was not enough foreshadowing and thus would have been only for shock value and angst.
