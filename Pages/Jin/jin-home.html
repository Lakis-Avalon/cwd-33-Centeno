JIN SECT

Jin Sect is one of the five great clans in the cultivation world. Their motif mainly consists of yellow robes and putting a vermillion dot on their foreheads. Additionally, the symbol of the sect is the white peony called "Sparks Amidst Snow". They are the wealthiest among the sects.

Location

It is evident from the imposing and blatant display of wealth in the architecture of Koi Tower that the Jin Sect is overflowing in wealth. They often host important events such as banquets and discussion conferences. There are murals in their paths with stories about the Jin Sect as well as distinguished cultivators. There is also a vault containing valuable items such as Nie Mingjue's head adventure Wen Ruohan's dagger.

Notable Figures

Jin Guangshan was a known womanizer in the cultivation world. He often spends his time with prostitutes and being indolent. Despite this, he is a great politician and a very cunning man. During the Sunshot Campaign, the main members from the start from the Jin Sect was his son Jin Zixuan. He was waiting for the decided victor of the Campaign and when it was clear that the alliance to take down the Wens was the victor, he swooped in and joined. He capitalised on the fact that his bastard son was the one who gave a death blow to Wen Ruohan. After this, he acknowledged Meng Yao but gave him the name of his generation and not his legitimate son's, disqualifying him for succession.

After the deaths of Jin Zixuan and Jin Zixun, only Meng Yao, now Jin Guangyao was the sole successor and he succeeded Jin Guangshan after his death. Jin Guangyao managed the sect well and became loved by the people for a time because of his construction of watch towers which helped noncultivators and people that are too far from sects to seek help.

Cultivation & Unique Practices

The Jin's cultivation and martial prowess were rarely shown during the Sunshot Campaign. Their archery skills could be considered decent as their heir Jin Zixuan was adept with a bow as well as Jin Ling. The Sect Leader Lianfang-zun, Jin Guangyao who dealt the killing blow to Wen Ruohan during the Sunshot Campaign did not specialize in confrontational battles, instead he is sneaky. His weapons included a softsword which is concealed by wrapping it around the waist and sharp threads that can be used in decapitation.

In the Jin Sect, what is noteworthy is the politicking within its walls. They incite discord between their enemies while doing underhanded work. The Jin condemned Wei Wuxian's cultivation which won them the war while they themselves housed a demonic cultivator trying to recreate Wei Wuxian's cultivation. The Jin Sect was also responsible for the camps of the Wen Remnants after the war where they were abused mercilessly, including old people and children.
