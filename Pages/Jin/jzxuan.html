Jin Zixuan

Jin Zixuan, the legitimate son of Jin Guangshan and Madam Jin, was the heir to the Lanling Jin Clan. Raised in a privileged environment, he bore the expectations of his sect while dealing with the shadow of his father's notorious reputation as a womanizer. Betrothed to Jiang Yanli of the Yunmeng Jiang Clan, Jin Zixuan initially opposed the arrangement, considering Jiang Yanli unworthy. After insulting her, Wei Wuxian fought him in her defense, leading to the dissolution of the engagement.

During the Sunshot Campaign, Jin Zixuan worked alongside his sect but continued to display arrogance, particularly toward Jiang Yanli. When she quietly supported the Jin forces by preparing meals, he mistakenly attributed the effort to another woman and accused her of seeking credit, causing further tensions. Wei Wuxian intervened and attacked him, worsening their strained relationship.

After the campaign, Jin Zixuan reflected on his actions and sought to repair his relationship with Jiang Yanli. His awkward but sincere efforts eventually led to their reconciliation, culminating in their marriage. Together, they had a son, Jin Ling, bringing joy to both families. Tragedy struck during Jin Ling's 100-day celebration. Amid tensions with Wei Wuxian and the cultivation sects, Jin Zixuan tried to approach Wei Wuxian, who was in a dangerous state. Wen Ning, perceiving him as a threat under Wei Wuxian's spiritual influence, accidentally killed him. Jin Zixuan's death marked a turning point in the escalating conflict and left a lasting impact on his family and sect.
