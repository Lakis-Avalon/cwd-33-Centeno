FORMER JIN SECT LEADER

Jin Guangyao

Jin Guangyao

Meng Yao, born the illegitimate son of Jin Guangshan and a prostitute, endured a harsh and stigmatized upbringing. Despite his humble origins, he was intelligent and ambitious, striving to rise above the societal scorn directed at him. However, his father, Jin Guangshan, refused to acknowledge him, forcing Meng Yao to carve out his own path. During the rise of the Wen Clan, Meng Yao met Lan Xichen and helped him hide from the Wens, beginning a bond between the two. His abilities and determination were eventually recognized by Nie Mingjue, who acknowledged Meng Yao's talents despite initial prejudice. Meng Yao's strategic mind earned him a place working alongside Nie Mingjue and Lan Xichen during the Sunshot Campaign. Their collaboration, though marked by mutual distrust on Nie Mingjue's part, became crucial to the war effort. At one point, Nie Mingjue even recommended Meng Yao to Jin Guangshan, believing his father could use someone with Meng Yao's skills—though Meng Yao saw no respite since he couldn't raise above his station just by being in the Jin Clan.

Meng Yao's darker nature began to emerge when he murdered Jin Clan disciples who had taken advantage of him. He used a Wen Clan sword to carry out the act and was discovered by Nie Mingjue. When confronted, Meng Yao refused to admit his crimes, and Nie Mingjue, enraged, threatened to kill him. Meng Yao managed to escape by incapacitating Nie Mingjue. During the Sunshot Campaign, Meng Yao served as a spy, sending critical intel about the Wen Clan's movements to Lan Xichen. However, his actions became increasingly complex when he allied himself with Wen Ruohan and became his trusted right-hand man. Nie Mingjue was ambushed due to false intelligence, and he discovered Meng Yao had betrayed their cause. After humiliating him in front of Wen Ruohan and killing his disciples, Meng Yao helped Nie Mingjue escape Nightless City.

Meng Yao ultimately turned on Wen Ruohan, stabbing him in the back and securing the Jin Clan's victory in the campaign. Following the war, Jin Guangshan finally acknowledged him as his son, reaping the benefits of Meng Yao's accomplishments without offering any paternal support. Now known as Jin Guangyao, Meng Yao rose to prominence within the Jin Clan but engaged in increasingly sinister activities. These included demonic cultivation experiments with Xue Yang, framing Wei Wuxian for various misdeeds, and orchestrating the death of Nie Mingjue by playing a harmful version of a Lan Clan song. Years after his initial rise, Jin Guangyao's schemes were unraveled. Thirteen years post-campaign, it was revealed that he had dismembered Nie Mingjue's corpse to create a fierce corpse even more powerful than Wen Ning, the Ghost General. His secrets were exposed, and his crimes—both against individuals and the cultivation world—made him a public enemy. In his final moments, Meng Yao realized that Nie Huaisang, Nie Mingjue's brother, had orchestrated his downfall from the shadows. Before his death, he expressed that despite all his terrible deeds, Lan Xichen was the one person he never thought of harming, seeing him as his only exception. Jin Guangyao died hated by many, leaving behind a legacy of treachery and tragedy.

Trivias

Jin Guangyao
A Name that is Scraps in Exchange for Meng Yao's Work

When Jin Guangyao was exalted as a war hero, Jin Guangshan acknowledged him, but his name says the opposite. Jin Zixuan and Jin Zixun same generation contemporaries of Jin Guangyao, start their names with the character 'Zi', unlike Jin Guangyao, starting with 'Guang' like his father, putting him in an awkward position and essentially removing him from line of succession.

Same Date
Same Date Different Treatment

Jin Guangyao and Jin Zixuan, ironically were both born on the same day. When Meng Yao was trying to meet Jin Guangshan on the date of his birth, people were celebrating Jin Zixuan's birth. Meng Yao on the other hand, was thrown from the stairs by the Jin on the day of his birth, after his mother died.

Trash Father
Meng Yao and His Mother

What broke Meng Yao was Jin Guangshan's slight against his mother. One time, when Jin Guangshan was hanging out with prostitutes, he ended up talking about Meng Shi, Meng Yao's mother while Jin Guangyao was present. Essentially, he was talking about Meng Shi like she was just troublesome for being able to read, an afterthought, nothing. His mother who waited for Jin Guangshan until her death, just nothing, someone his father barely remembered. Him and his mother were nothing to Jin Guangshan, he would never be loved. So he orchestrated his death by being serviced by prostitutes till he died, fitting for scum like Jin Guangshan.

Unexpected Mercy
A Single Mercy Does not Absolve Crimes, but is Mercy Nonetheless

Of course, Jin Guangyao killed the prostitutes he used to kill Jin Guangshan, except one. This one prostitute contributed to his downfall, she is named Sisi. Wei Wuxian saw the memory from a flashback that Sisi helped him and his mother when they were being harrassed, concluding that this might be the reason he spared her.
