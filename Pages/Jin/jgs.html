FORMER JIN SECT LEADER

Jin Guangshan

"Since the Yunmeng Jiang Sect and my sect share an amicable relation, I'll give you an advice. Relinquish the Stygian Tiger Amulet and the remnants of the Wen Sect."

Jin Guangshan

Jin Guangshan, the patriarch of the Lanling Jin Clan, was a cunning and self-serving man, infamous for his moral corruption. Known for his rampant womanizing, he fathered numerous illegitimate children, including Jin Guangyao, whom he only acknowledged after the Sunshot Campaign for political gain. Despite his authority, he lacked genuine leadership qualities, manipulating others and prioritizing his own benefits over ethics.

During the Sunshot Campaign, Jin Guangshan was reluctant going against the Wen Clan but was quick to claim credit for the success brought by others, particularly Wei Wuxian and his innovations. He exploited Meng Yao's cunning and resourcefulness while treating him with contempt due to his illegitimacy.Jin Guangshan's machinations extended to fostering conflict among the sects and plotting against Wei Wuxian, whom he scapegoated for his clan's schemes.Ultimately, his underhanded deeds and disregard for loyalty led to his downfall. Jin Guangshan died ignominiously, dying from being serviced by prostitutes courtesy of Jin Guangyao
