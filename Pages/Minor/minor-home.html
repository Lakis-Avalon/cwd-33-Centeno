ROGUES & MINOR SECTS

Some cultivators do not have the luxury of the benefits brought about by being part of a great sect. Those in minor sects tend to be subsidiary sects for the major ones. Rogue cultivators on the other hand have more freedom, but the risks and chances of death are greater while the rewards are often meagre. Some also tend to have unorthodox cultivation in their arsenal ranging from creating ice to controlling resentful energy. They often live by their wits, adapting to survive without the resources of the major clans.

Location

These cultivators lack grand bases like the major clans. They may operate from temporary camps, small villages, or hidden caves. Minor sects often have modest headquarters, such as simple halls or shrines. Their homes are usually practical and tailored to their local environment, with limited resources compared to the wealthier clans. Some tend to be wanderers going from place to place looking to help the common people.

Notable Figures

Wei Wuxian's mother, Cangse Sanren was a renowned cultivator because she was a disciple of an immortal named Baoshan Sanren. She also has a martial brother named Xiao Xingchen who had a dream with his dear friend, Song Zichen. They each had a title, "Xiao Xingchen of the Bright Moon and Gentle Breeze" and "Song Zichen, He Who Stands Proud Amidst the Snow and Frost". Essentially, this means that Xiao Xingchen was pure and Song Zichen was someone who thrives despite hardship.

On the other hand, there is a demonic cultivator who raised and experimented with the dead called Xue Chengmei. He massacred a whole sect because in his childhood, he lost his pinkie due to the sect's leader's actions. He tried to replicate Wei Wuxian's path as well as his inventions.

Cultivation & Unique Practices

Cultivators that are not part of the great sects could be very creative in their methods. Xiao Xingchen for example has a spiritual sword called Shuanghua which is able to detect corpse energy. It cannot distinguish between the living and the dead though, which Xue Yang took advantage of to trick him into killing normal people. His partner, Song Zichen uses a horsetail whisk on top of his cultivation sword.

Xue Chengmei was taken in by the Jin Sect at some point in time because he can imitate Wei Wuxian's cultivation to an extent. He is quite imaginative in his own right. He was able to make skull-piercing nails that allowed him to control Wen Qionglin. He also doused common living people in corpse powder and cut off their tongues then made the blind Xiao Xingchen kill them with Shuanghua, an attestment to his vindictive and cruel nature.
