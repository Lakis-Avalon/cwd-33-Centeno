BALING OUYANG SECT HEIR

Ouyang Zizhen

"Not bad. You can tell that she was a beauty from just one glance. You've got sharp eyes and a unique sense. I'm sure you'll be a romantic in the future."

Ouyang Zizhen

Ouyang Zizhen is the young heir of the Baling Ouyang Sect, a minor sect among the cultivation world. He is a kind, observant, and courageous individual with a romantic and emotional nature. Wei Wuxian once noted that Zizhen would likely grow up to be someone deeply romantic, a sentiment reflected in his sensitivity and ability to empathize with others. During the events in Yi City, Zizhen demonstrated his emotional depth when he cried for Xiao Xingchen and A-Qing's tragic fate, showcasing his compassionate and tender-hearted personality. His attentiveness also shone through when he noticed A-Qing's ghost and remarked on her beauty, reflecting his keen observational skills.

Despite being a bit emotional, Zizhen is also brave and stands up for what he believes in. He courageously opposed his father and other sect leaders when they accused Wei Wuxian of kidnapping them to the Burial Mounds, firmly stating that Wei Wuxian had saved them instead. His loyalty and sense of justice were evident in his refusal to blindly follow the prejudices of others. Zizhen is close friends with Lan Sizhui, Lan Jingyi, and Jin Ling, often accompanying them on adventures and forming a strong camaraderie within the younger generation. He greatly admires both Wei Wuxian and Lan Wangji, looking up to them as role models for their abilities and moral character.
