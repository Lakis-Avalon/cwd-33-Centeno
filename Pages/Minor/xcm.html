Xue Chengmei

Xue Yang's life was defined by cruelty, revenge, and manipulation. As a child, he was an orphan mistreated for his background. His hatred for the Chang Clan began when their servants crushed his pinky finger during a beating, an event that left him physically and emotionally scarred. As an adult, Xue Yang mastered demonic cultivation and annihilated the Chang Clan in revenge, gaining notoriety for his brutality.

After the massacre, Xue Yang was captured by Xiao Xingchen and Song Zichen and asked for Xue Yang to be executed. However, the Jin Clan released him, intending to use his abilities. Following his release, Xue Yang retaliated by massacring Song Zichen's sect, Baixue Temple, and blinding Song Zichen in the process. Xiao Xingchen gave his own eyes to Song Zichen and disappeared from his life.

Later, Xue Yang encountered Xiao Xingchen again when Jin Guangyao betrayed him, though Xiao Xingchen did not recognize him. Xiao Xingchen, kind by nature, helped the injured man he knew nothing of who he found on the road to Yi City. They lived together for two years, during which Xue Yang manipulated Xiao Xingchen into unknowingly killing innocent people disguised as fierce corpses. This manipulation culminated in Xiao Xingchen killing Song Zichen, believing him to be an enemy.At the same time, Xue Yang's identity was revealed and Xue Yang mocked him saying that all the things he did, helping people, was for naught and the nail in the coffin was when Xue Yang let Xiao Xingchen know that the one he just killed was Song Zichen. In blood of tears, Xue Yang slit his throat, he was so devastated even Xue Yang's skills cannot revive him.After Xiao Xingchen's death, Xue Yang kept his body and impersonated him, clinging to the memory of the rare kindness Xiao Xingchen had shown him. Xue Yang eventually died during the events in Yi City, clutching a piece of candy Xiao Xingchen had once given him, a reminder of the only kindness he ever experienced.

Trivias

His Name
Irony

Xue Yang's surname, 薛, is the top half of another character, 孽 (niè), which means sin, or evil. His courtesy name 薛成美 (Xuē Chéngměi) is derived from the proverb 成人之美 (chéng rén zhī měi) meaning 'help others succeed' and is usually used to describe the conduct of a gentleman.

Gap Moe
Apple-Cutting

He is known to cut apples into rabbit shapes.
