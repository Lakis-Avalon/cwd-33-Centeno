Song Zichen, He Who Stands Proud Amidst the Snow and Frost

Song Zichen, courtesy name Song Lan, was a righteous cultivator from Baixue Temple and a close friend of Xiao Xingchen. The two shared a vision of building a sect based on shared ideals rather than blood ties. Their friendship turned tragic when Xue Yang, seeking revenge on Xiao Xingchen for capturing him after the Yueyang Chang Clan massacre, targeted Baixue Temple. Xue Yang slaughtered the sect and blinded Song Lan with poison. Xiao Xingchen, devastated, gave his eyes to Song Lan but was rejected by his grieving friend, who told him they should no longer meet.

After coming back from Baoshan Sanren's mountain where Xiao Xingchen brought him to heal, Song Lan sought revenge but was unable to act, as the Jin Clan already 'executed' Xue Yang. Eventually, Song Lan located Xiao Xingchen in Yi City but unknowingly walked into Xue Yang's trap. In a confrontation with Xue Yang, Song Lan was killed and turned into a Fierce Corpse, manipulated to fight Xiao Xingchen, who unknowingly killed him. When Xiao Xingchen learned the truth, he took his own life in despair.

Song Lan, controlled by Xue Yang, later attacked Wei Wuxian and the others in Yi City. After Wei Wuxian freed him by removing control nails from his head, Song Lan regained his consciousness but remained haunted by his trauma. He chose to roam the world, carrying the fragmented souls of Xiao Xingchen and A-Qing, hoping to restore them one day.
