LAN SECT

The Lan Sect was founded by Lan An who was previously a monk before he found his cultivation partner and they then established the sect together. The sect is held in high regard by the rest of the cultivation world for their rich collection of knowledge and writings in their libraries. They obediently follow the rules that they made which emphasize morality, discipline, and self-restraint, reflecting the sect's values of righteousness and virtue.

Location

Cloud Recesses is the place where this sect resides and it is in the region of Gusu. This is a mountainous region with quite a cold climate. The rules of the sect are expected to be observed within the Cloud Recesses by cultivators that attend lectures here. There are restrictions for alcohol, a strict schedule for sleeping, and regulated number of meals for example for the guest cultivators to remember and follow during their stay. There is also an abundance of rabbits in the backhills of Cloud Recesses thanks to Lan Wangji taking care of the bunnies gifted to him by Wei Wuxian.

Notable Figures

The Sect Leader was originally Lan Wangji and Lan Xichen's father, Qingheng-jun. However, their mother killed an elder of the sect so in order to protect her from execution, Qingheng-jun married her and fended off those asking for her death. As a result of this event, Qingheng-jun went into seclusion, and Madam Lan was confined in the Gentian House. They then eventually had Lan Wangji and Lan Xichen and Qingheng-jun's brother, Lan Qiren would bring the children to visit their mother once a month. Lan Qiren then stepped up as the acting Sect Leader, managing the sect until Xichen came of age.

Cultivation & Unique Practices

The Lan Sect specializes in spiritual cultivation through music, meditation, and scholarly study. The motif of the clothes worn by cultivators belonging to this sect are white and blue with clouds. Members also wear head ribbons which symbolizes restraint and only family or cultivation partners of the cultivator can touch them. Those in the main family of the sect have clouds imprinted on their white ribbons.

Their use of music is very versatile, able to bring devastating damage, cleanse resentful energy, or communicate with spirits. During the Sunshot Campaign, a strum of a guqin by Lan Wangji was able to stop a barrage of arrows aimed toward their base. Throughout their adventure with solving the case of the dismembered fierce corpse, the use of their musical cultivation was very helpful. The Lan use a song called 'Inquiry' which is used to ask spirits for information, they used this to locate Jin Ling who was trapped in the Nie Sect Ancestral Hall. In order to suppress the fierce corpse of Nie Mingjue, Wei Wuxian, Lan Wangji, and Lan Xichen also played music together to cleanse the body of resentful energy.
