NIE SECT CULTIVATOR

Nie Mingjue

"Countless souls have fallen under my sabre. But I have never killed out of my own desires, let alone for power."

Nie Mingjue, Chifeng-zun

Wen Ruohan caused the death of the previous Sect Leader Nie by sabotaging his sabre which caused Nie Mingjue to take up the mantle of sect leader early on in life. He is very strict with his brother and repeatedly sends him to Cloud Recesses to learn from the Lan. He was not very much interested in things outside of his training with the sabre.

During the Sunshot Campaign, he lead the front in Hejian where he defeated Wen Xu and beheaded him. He meets Jin Guangyao there and they had a friendly relationship. He helped Meng Yao by giving him a recommendation letter to use on the Jin Sect. He eventually learns that rather than going to the Jin Clan, he saw Meng Yao kill a Jin Cultivator with Wen techniques. He tried to kill him, but Meng Yao escapes. Throughout the campaign, he gets information from Lan Xichen's spy which led him to many victories. However, there came a time where it was false information and they get captured. It turns out Meng Yao was now the right hand man of Wen Ruohan. He killed many of Nie Mingjue's subordinates and asked Wen Ruohan to torture him instead of killing him. Nie Mingue wakes up from fainting to find Meng Yao getting him out of there and they meet Lan Xichen which reveals his identity as the spy.

After Sunshot, he reluctantly joins a sworn brotherhood with Lan Xichen and Meng Yao, now named Jin Guangyao despite his distrust for the former. He is aware of the atrocities committed by the Jin Clan and Jin Guangyao. He condemned the Wen Remnants for not doing anything against Wen Ruohan while not calling out the Jins lying about their abuse in concentration camps. In the Massacre of Nightless City where thousands died, including Nie disciples, Nie Mingjue was angered. He later joins the First Siege of the Burial Mounds and partakes in slaughtering the Wen Remnants.

Eventually he dies because of Jin Guangyao taking advantage of the music piece Xichen gave in hopes to help Nie Mingjue. He becomes a dismembered fierce corpse which Lan Wangji and Wei Wuxian try to identify the identity of. He became a fierce corpse that seeks to kill those of Jin blood, evident in it targeting Jin Ling and Wei Wuxian in Mo Xuanyu's body. In Guanyin Temple, he ends up getting sealed inside the coffin with Jin Guangyao.

    Nie Mingjue in Action

    Nie Mingjue was one of the vital forces in Sunshot Campaign. He beheaded Wen Xu, Wen Ruohan's eldest child. His sabre's name is Baxia. In the latter parts of the Sunshot Campaign, he gets captured by Wen Ruohan. Jin Guangyao, Meng Yao at the time helped him escape and kill Wen Ruohan.

Strongest Corpse

Nie Mingjue is a stronger fierce corpse than Wen Ning, the Ghost General. The difference, however, is that Wen Ning retains his cosciousness unlike Nie Mingjue who is essentially a ridiculously strong fierce corpse with no sentience.
