NIE SECT

The ancestors of the Nie Sect were butchers and they specialize in using the sabre. They imbue the sabres with resentful energy from animals. This use of resentful energy lead to untimely deaths spanning for generations.

Location

Located in the mountainous region of Qinghe, the Unclean Realm is a fortress-like stronghold. It reflects the Nie clan's militant nature, with sturdy architecture and minimal ornamentation. The base is both a defensive structure and a training ground for warriors. The Nies also have an ancestral hall which is vital in their cultivation and family secret.

Notable Figures

The leader of the Nie Sect died because of Wen Ruohan. Nie Mingjue was forced to take the mantle of being a sect leader at a young age. The heirs of the sect are the half-brothers Nie Mingjue and Nie Huaisang. Unlike Nie Mingjue who was remarkably skilled with the sabre as observed in his fight with Wen Ruohan, Nie Huaisang was considered incompetent in a lot of things. His interests originally were in the arts but Nie Mingjue's early death made him grow up quickly.

Cultivation And Unique Practices

Unlike other cultivators who use double edged swords, those in the Nie sect used sabres. Unlike Wei Wuxian who used resentful energy of humans, their cultivation only used resentful energy from animals which they imbue in their blades. This buildup of resentful energy made the users of this cultivation have untimely and early deaths due to their tendency to have a qi deviation easily.

Upon their deaths, the resentment-filled sabres of the dead Nie Sect members are then placed in their Ancestral Halls. They trap these sabres with multiple fierce corpses in order to "neutralize" them. The sabres and the fierce corpses are forced to fight within the Ancestral Hall. This is a closely guarded secret of the Nie Sect.
